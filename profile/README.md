## 🖌 Overview  
Capture One Advanced is an industry-leading RAW photo editing application trusted by commercial photographers, fashion studios, and photo professionals worldwide for its exceptional color science, tethered shooting capabilities, and non-destructive editing workflow.

The color grading system gives you unmatched control over how RAW data is interpreted and rendered. Color balance, color editor, and skin tone tools work together to produce accurate, natural color that reflects the photographer's vision rather than a generic profile.

Tethered shooting connects directly to supported cameras, displaying captures on screen the moment the shutter fires. The live preview and instant review workflow is the standard in commercial and studio photography for a reason.

Layered masking with AI-powered subject selection, luminance range, and color range tools makes local adjustments precise and non-destructive, letting you target exactly the region that needs treatment without affecting the rest of the image.

For professional photographers who demand the highest quality RAW processing and the most capable studio workflow tool available, Capture One Advanced is the editing platform that defines the professional standard.

## 🚀 Why Choose Capture One Advanced?

Capture One Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Capture One Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
